Hi, I'm Kat!

A web developer and graphic designer.
I have a background in public relations and marketing, with a degree in Journalism and Public Relations from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am now pursuing my second degree, in Web Development and Design.
My design style is clean and modern. I think design should be thoughtful and purposeful – it should speak to you and all your senses. I’m inspired not only by visual arts and typography, but all other types of media as well. l also love to cook, and I always say we eat with our eyes first. Therefore presentation is so important! And I think it can be fun — and individualized — and spectacular.
Please take a look at my portfolio – I certainly hope you like what you see!
